Rohit Gurunath SharmaRohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the Indian national team across all formats. He is a right-handed batsman, considered one of the best of his generation and one of the greatest opening batters of all time. Rohit Sharma was born in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India, on April 30, 1987. He made his international debut in 2007 and has since played in over 400 matches across all formats. He is a prolific scorer, with over 23,000 runs in international cricket. Rohit Sharma is also a successful captain. He has led Mumbai Indians to five IPL titles, the most by any captain in the tournament's history. He was appointed captain of the Indian national team in 2019 and has led the team to victory in several major tournaments, including the 2020 Asia Cup and the 2023 World Cup. Rohit Sharma is a popular figure in India and is considered one of the best cricketers in the world. He is admired for his talent, sportsmanship, and leadership qualities.
